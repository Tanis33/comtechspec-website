About Us

Our Clients

ComTech Specialists proudly partners with small businesses across a variety of industries, with a special focus on accounting firms. Our clients value the personalized attention and expert guidance we bring to their IT challenges. From streamlining workflows to safeguarding sensitive financial data, we understand the unique demands of accounting and work tirelessly to deliver solutions that meet those needs. Whether it’s a startup or an established firm, our clients rely on us to keep their technology running smoothly, enabling them to thrive in today’s competitive environment.

Our Mission

At ComTech Specialists, our mission is to empower small businesses with seamless, secure, and innovative IT solutions. We are dedicated to simplifying technology so our clients can focus on what they do best: running their businesses. By combining personalized support with cutting-edge tools, we aim to deliver peace of mind and maximize productivity for accounting firms and other small enterprises. Our commitment is to foster growth, reliability, and success for every client we serve.

Our Story

Founded over 20 years ago in Greensboro, NC, ComTech Specialists has been a trusted partner for small businesses seeking reliable IT solutions. What began as a passion for technology and a commitment to customer service has grown into a thriving managed service provider (MSP) dedicated to helping accounting firms and other small businesses navigate the ever-evolving tech landscape. We take pride in building lasting relationships with our clients, tailoring our services to meet their unique needs. Through decades of innovation and expertise, we’ve established ourselves as a cornerstone of IT excellence in the local community.
